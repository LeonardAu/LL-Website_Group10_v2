QUARTER 3:

LIVE STREAMING & ANALYSIS

Topic

Self-Defense and how it affects Confidence Podcast

WonderMinds

Watch our stream:

This is our official Live Stream podcast, so sit back and watch!
You can also find it on Facebook by pressing the button below.

Summary of Stream

Over the past few quarters, we've been getting ready to launch our stream. We've been learning all about it and figuring out how to do it. Before we actually launched, we made a promotional poster and a video alongside to share on social media to get people aware of our upcomming stream. We also played around with the streaming software to figure out what would look good on the stream. Once we were ready, we launched the stream and talked about our topic which was self-defense. We interacted with the audience and explained what we were doing. We're happy to say that we hit our target with the stream so far. After we finished the stream, we asked people to take a survey and tell us how they liked it.

01.

Before the Stream

Before the stream actually started on February 7, 2023, at 3 p.m, the streamers planned to post a promotional video on January 23, 2023, to announce and inform the followers of the topic of the stream, the date, the social media of the channel, and the website.

02.

During the Stream

During the stream, We discussed the significance of self-defense for teenagers. We defined and demonstrated self-defense, highlighted its importance in daily life, and explained how it can boost teenagers' confidence and skill development.

03.

After the Stream

Following the live stream, we wanted to gather feedback from our viewers, so we distributed a survey to know their thoughts and opinions about the stream. We value our audience's feedback, and their responses will help us to improve our future streams, ensuring that we meet their needs and expectations.
